Welcome to Crowdocs!

Project "Crowdocs" (try to) help people in collaborative creativity (for less then a week) by suggesting convinient platform for editing text, articles and posts!

You can register on the site and add your articles to sections by topic, as well as create your own topics. You can also share the link to the article with your friends and other users, suggesting that they edit the article or add new information to it. With crowdocs, students can work together, learn together, and create their own unique text. This is very convenient, because you can use the site to see which user last edited the article and which text was added.
